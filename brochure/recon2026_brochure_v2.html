Section 01 — About the Workshop
Objectives & Relevance

◎ Workshop Objectives

  • Review recent research advances in Systems & Software Security and their implications for modern computing platforms
  • Analyze system-level security architectures and examine emerging challenges including supply-chain trust, post-quantum threats, and AI-assisted attacks
  • Identify security threats, attack surfaces, and trust assumptions in complex software-driven and large-scale distributed systems
  • Facilitate brainstorming on research challenges aligned with national cybersecurity priorities

⚑ Target Audience

  • Faculty working in Systems & Software Security
  • PhD scholars and research scholars in cybersecurity
  • PG students specializing in embedded systems and secure architectures
  • Industry researchers in systems, software, and cybersecurity
  • Professionals in Security Operations Centres (SOC) and threat intelligence

Section 04 — Expected Outcomes
Research Impact

The workshop is designed to produce lasting research outcomes that strengthen India's Systems & Software Security research capacity, aligned with the national cybersecurity mission under MeitY's ISEA Project.

🔬 Research Outcomes

  • Identification of key research challenges in secure system architectures, OS security, and resilient distributed computing
  • Well-defined research problem statements from structured brainstorming on secure software frameworks and system-level threat detection
  • Creation of a collaborative research roadmap for Systems & Software Security innovation
  • Encouragement of academic publications and new research proposals in the thematic area

🤝 Collaboration & Capacity

  • Enhanced collaboration between academic institutions and industry partners for joint research initiatives
  • Strengthened research capacity among faculty and PhD scholars through exposure to recent advances and open challenges
  • Increased awareness of threats in distributed systems, cloud platforms, IoT, embedded software, and cyber-physical systems
  • Development of a skilled research community for advanced security challenges in modern computing

◎ National Cybersecurity Ecosystem Impact

The workshop will directly contribute to strengthening the national cybersecurity ecosystem by fostering knowledge exchange between academia, industry experts, and researchers in systems security, software security, and cyber-physical infrastructure protection — core priorities under India's national cybersecurity mission.
