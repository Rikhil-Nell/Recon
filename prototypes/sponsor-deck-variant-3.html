Event Highlights

Beyond Traditional Conferences

Recon 2026 combines competitive hacking, hands-on learning, and professional networking in a 72-hour security immersion.

  • 48-Hour CTF Marathon: Jeopardy-style challenges across web exploitation, cryptography, forensics, reverse engineering, and binary exploitation with live leaderboards
  • King of the Hill Competition: Attack/defend real-world vulnerable infrastructure in teams, maintain persistence while defending against competitors
  • Security Villages: Dedicated zones for hardware hacking, wireless security, OSINT, cloud pentesting, malware analysis, and lockpicking with expert guidance
  • Technical Workshops: Interactive sessions on threat modeling, secure code review, incident response, and bug bounty hunting led by industry practitioners
  • Lightning Talks: 15-minute presentations from students and professionals on novel exploits, tool releases, and research findings
